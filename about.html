About Pro Apple Repair Center

With over 10 years of experience, we are your trusted experts for all Apple device repairs.

Our Story

Founded in 2013, Pro Apple Repair Center started with a simple mission: to provide fast, reliable, and affordable repair services for Apple devices. What began as a small repair shop with two technicians has now grown into a full-service repair center with a team of certified experts.

We've repaired over 25,000 devices and counting, earning the trust of our community with our commitment to quality workmanship and exceptional customer service.

Our team stays up-to-date with the latest Apple technologies and repair techniques through continuous training, ensuring we can handle even the newest devices with precision.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the highest quality Apple device repairs while delivering exceptional customer service. We believe in transparency, fair pricing, and using only genuine or high-quality parts that meet or exceed Apple's standards.

We're committed to:

  • Using only high-quality, reliable parts
  • Providing accurate diagnostics and honest recommendations
  • Completing repairs in a timely manner
  • Offering competitive pricing with no hidden fees
  • Standing behind our work with a comprehensive warranty
