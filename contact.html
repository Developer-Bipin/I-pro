Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical repair take?

Most common repairs like screen replacements or battery changes are completed within 1-2 hours while you wait. More complex repairs like water damage or logic board issues may take 24-48 hours.

Do you offer warranty on repairs?

Yes, all repairs come with a 90-day warranty covering both parts and labor. If the same issue reoccurs within this period, we'll fix it free of charge.

Do I need an appointment?

Walk-ins are welcome, but we recommend scheduling an appointment to minimize wait times. You can schedule online through our contact form or by calling us directly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, Apple Pay, and cash. We do not accept personal checks.
